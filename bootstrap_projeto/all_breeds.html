Border Collie

The Border Collie is a highly intelligent and energetic dog breed, renowned for its exceptional herding abilities and agility.

  • Border Collies are a social breed and require a lot of attention from their owners. They can be destructive or develop behavioral issues when left alone without mental and physical stimulation.
  • high energy levels and require a significant amount of physical activity each day
  • Joint problems, hip dysplasia, Collie Eye Anomaly (CEA)
