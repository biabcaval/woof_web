What is woof?

Woof is a project with the goal of finding forever homes for rescued dogs which were abandoned. We already saved more than 50 dogs lives and we aim to save more and more. We are a non-profit organization and we are always looking for volunteers to help us with the dogs. You too can help a dog find a home sharing our website with your friends and family and donating money to help maintaining the dogs.
